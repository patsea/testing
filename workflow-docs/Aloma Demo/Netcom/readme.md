# Netcom Lead Automation Workflow

**Automates multi-source lead capture, normalisation, enrichment, and multi-channel notification with AI and CRM integration.**

---

## What This Workflow Does

This workflow orchestrates the end-to-end process of ingesting leads from various sources (webhooks, forms, CRMs), normalising and enriching the data, and distributing it to multiple destinations such as HubSpot, Apollo, Airtable, ServiceNow, Google Docs, Slack, and email. It leverages AI (OpenAI, Claude) for content generation and supports advanced routing based on lead attributes (e.g., job title, premium status). Ideal for teams needing robust, code-first automation of lead management, enrichment, and cross-system notification.
